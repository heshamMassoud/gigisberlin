Our Story

For us, pizza has never been about trends or a fancy hype. It's about people, connection, and the simple joy of sharing a meal in a place that feels real. That's why we started Gigi's, to bring our neighborhood's feeling here in Berlin. A spot with soul, comfort, and personality, inspired by the authentic traditions of Naples.

Gigi's is meant to be your go-to place. Whether you're celebrating something great, need a little comfort after a long day, coming solo, with a partner, or your loud group of friends. Our ovens are hot, and there's always a seat (and a warm welcome) waiting for you.

From Our Kitchen

A taste of what awaits you

Antipasti

Marinated olives with warm, hand-pulled house bread, golden frittelle, creamy burrata, and boards of San Daniele prosciutto, mortadella & Salame Napoli.

Pizza Napoletana

San Marzano tomatoes, fior di latte and wild basil over a slow-risen base — Marinara, Margherita, Capricciosa, La Norma and Provola e Pepe. Every pizza available vegan.

🍸

Cocktails & Aperitivo

Gigi's Negroni, Espresso Martini and Basil Smash, alongside Aperol Spritz, Bellini and a short list of Southern Italian wines by the glass.
